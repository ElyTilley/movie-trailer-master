Big Hero 6

Directors: Don Hall, Chris Williams

PG

1h 42min

Hiro Hamada is a fourteen year old super genius with a knack for bot fighting. When his older brother Tadashi dies in a accident, Hiro comes face to face with a villin wearing a Kabuki Mask and not all seems to be as it looks. Hiro, along with his brother's robot Baymax and their friends Gogo, Honeylemon, Wassabi and Fred come together to investigate more about the accident that took Tadashi's life and find out what this man in a Kabuki mask has to do with all of this.
